Frequently Asked Questions

What is EduLink?

EduLink is an online learning management platform where students can enroll in courses, access materials, participate in quizzes, and collaborate in discussion forums. Instructors can manage courses, assignments, and track student progress.

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply sign up for an account, browse through the course catalog, and click "Enroll Now" on your desired course. You’ll gain instant access to all course materials and resources.

Can I access EduLink on my mobile device?

Yes! EduLink is fully responsive and can be accessed on both desktop and mobile devices. This allows you to learn anytime, anywhere.

What if I need help with a course?

You can ask questions in the discussion forums or reach out directly to your instructor through the platform’s messaging feature. Additionally, our chatbot is always available to assist you with general questions.

How do I contact support?

For any technical issues or questions, you can reach our support team by clicking on the "Contact Us" link in the footer or by sending an email to support@edulink.com.
